# Code of Conduct

Simdock follows a simple contributor standard:

- Be respectful and specific.
- Critique code and design decisions, not people.
- Assume good intent, but clarify quickly when something is risky or unclear.
- Keep issues actionable.
- Do not harass, threaten, or exclude contributors.

Maintainers may close issues, edit comments, or block participants who repeatedly violate these expectations.
